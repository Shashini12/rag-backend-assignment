EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK 2026 - COMPLETE VERSION

SECTION 1: ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees receive 14 days of annual leave after completing one year of service.
Employees who have worked for more than 5 years receive 21 days of annual leave.
Annual leave must be requested at least two weeks in advance and approved by your manager.
Unused annual leave can be carried over to the next year, but maximum carryover is 10 days.
Part-time employees receive pro-rated annual leave based on their working hours.

SECTION 2: SICK LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees get 10 paid sick days per year.
Sick days cannot be carried over to the next year. Use them or lose them.
For sick leave lasting more than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.
Part-time employees receive sick leave pro-rated based on their standard work hours.

SECTION 3: WORK HOURS AND BREAKS
Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday, with a 1-hour unpaid lunch break.
The company observes the following holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, 
Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.
Employees are entitled to two 15-minute paid breaks per day.

SECTION 4: REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work requests must be submitted by Wednesday for the following week.
Employees working remotely must be available on company communication platforms from 9 AM to 5 PM.

SECTION 5: TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
The company provides  annually for professional development.
Training requests must be approved by the department head.
Employees are encouraged to attend at least one conference per year.

SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: June and December.
Reviews include self-assessment, manager feedback, and goal setting for the next period.
Salary adjustments are based on performance review outcomes and market conditions.

SECTION 7: CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must complete the annual ethics training.
Confidential company information must not be shared externally.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination.

SECTION 8: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the trip.
Meal reimbursement is limited to  per day.
Hotel expenses require prior approval for amounts exceeding  per night.

SECTION 9: PARENTAL LEAVE
New parents receive 12 weeks of paid parental leave.
Parental leave can be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.
Employees may request an additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave.

SECTION 10: TECHNOLOGY USE
Company laptops must have all security updates installed.
Personal use of company devices should be limited and appropriate.
All passwords must be changed every 90 days.
